Movie Tickets & Netflix Subscription Trend

In today's world, people have been shifting from going to theaters to using streaming platforms at home.
Consumers can to access their favorite shows more easily, without going to theaters or waiting for broadcasting schedule.

In the US, the number of movie tickets sold has been generally decreasing while netflix subscriptions have been increasing.

The gap between the two are growing even more due to COVID-19 pandemic making people stay at home.
